**ocs** is a full-screen fuzzy finder for local
[OpenCode](https://opencode.ai/){target="\_blank"} sessions.

It indexes session titles, project directories, IDs, and recent prompts, making
old sessions easy to find. The selected session is reopened in its original
directory, with a preview of its complete text-message transcript.
